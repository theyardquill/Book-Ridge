# The Seven Days of Creation
### A Literary Rendering

---

## Day I

**In the beginning**, the Elohim forged the heavens and the earth.

And the earth was *void*—formless and empty, wrapped in darkness upon the deep. And the Spirit of the 
Elohim *brooded* over the face of the waters.

Then the Elohim *spoke*:

> **"Let there be LIGHT!"**

And *behold*—light burst forth from the void.

The Elohim separated the light from the darkness. The light He called *Day*; the darkness He called 
*Night*.

And there was evening. And there was morning—the **first day**.

---

## Day II

And the Elohim spoke once more:

> **"Let there be a FIRMAMENT between the waters, dividing waters from waters!"**

And it was so.

The Elohim fashioned the firmament—*the great expanse*—and it divided the waters above from the waters 
below.

He called the firmament **Heaven**.

And there was evening. And there was morning—the **second day**.

---

## Day III

Then the Elohim commanded:

> **"Let the waters beneath the heavens be gathered into one place, and let DRY LAND appear!"**

And *behold*—the waters retreated, and the earth rose from the deep.

The Elohim called the dry land **Earth**, and the gathered waters **Seas**.

And He spoke:

> **"Let the earth bring forth VEGETATION—plants bearing seed, trees fruiting with seed within them!"**

And the earth obeyed. vegetation sprang forth—every manner of plant and herb, each according to its kind.

And there was evening. And there was morning—the **third day**.

---

## Day IV

The Elohim spoke again:

> **"Let there be LUMINARIES in the firmament of heaven—to separate day from night, to mark the seasons, 
days, and years!"**

And He made two great lights:

The **greater light** to rule the day,
The **lesser light** to rule the night.

He set them in the firmament—the stars also.

And there was evening. And there was morning—the **fourth day**.

---

## Day V

And the Elohim said:

> **"Let the waters SWARM with living creatures, and let BIRDS fly above the earth, across the firmament of 
heaven!"**

So He created the great sea-monsters and every living thing that moves—*myriads of creatures*—each 
according to its kind.

And every winged thing according to its kind.

And the Elohim *blessed* them, saying:

> **"Be fruitful, multiply, and fill of the seas; the waters let the birds multiply upon the earth!"**

And there was evening. And there was morning—the **fifth day**.

---

## Day VI

Then the Elohim spoke:

> **"Let the EARTH bring forth living creatures—each according to its kind: cattle, creeping things, beasts 
of the earth!"**

And it was so.

The earth brought forth living creatures—each according to its kind: the beasts, the cattle, and every 
creeping thing upon the ground.

And the Elohim *saw* that it was **good**.

Then the Elohim said:

> **"Let Us make MAN in Our image, after Our likeness..."**

And the Elohim created Adam—**in His own image**, in the image of the Elohim He created him;
**Male and female** He created them.

And the Elohim *blessed* them:

> **"Be fruitful, multiply, fill the earth, and SUB DUE it. Have dominion over the fish of the sea, the 
birds of heaven, and every living thing that moves upon the earth!"**

And the Elohim said:

> **"Behold! I have given you every plant yielding seed upon the face of the earth, and every tree with 
seed in its fruit—you shall have them for FOOD."**

And to every beast of the earth, every bird of heaven, and every creeping thing—*every green thing* for 
food.

And it was so.

And the Elohim *beheld* all that He had made, and—

> **BEHOLD!**
>
> **IT WAS EXCEEDINGLY GOOD.**

And there was evening. And there was morning—the **sixth day**.

---

## Day VII

**Thus the heavens and the earth were finished—all their vast array.**

And on the seventh day, the Elohim **rested** from all His work.

He *sanctified* the seventh day, for upon it He ceased from all the labor He had accomplished.

> **The Sabbath of the Lord.**

---

*Thus concludes the drama of Creation.*
